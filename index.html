About Crypto Lottery

Crypto Lottery was started in an attempt to create a more equal playing field for organising lotteries. Have you ever wondered why governments have the monopoly on lotteries? Well, mainly because they put around 50% of the winnings in their own pocket.

Crypto Lottery will put an end to this be creating a decentralized lottery run by a smart contract. This means that over time, when fully build out, close to 100% of contributions made will be part of the winnings. Creating an equal playing field without government interference. The first few rounds we will keep 25% of contributions to build out the infrastructure

How does it work?
